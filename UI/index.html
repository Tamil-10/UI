    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does the 30-day free trial work?

    When you sign up, you instantly get access to our fully-loaded Estate plan for 30 days. We will email you when your trial expires. You can then select a plan and pay by credit card to continue using the product. If you choose not to continue, your account will be suspended.

    How does the built-in phone work?

    Make and receive calls in Freshsales by purchasing local and toll-free numbers starting at $1/month. Once you enable the phone feature, you’ll receive an email along with a credit worth $5 to buy and make phone calls. You can always top up your credits from “Plans & Billing” in your Freshsales account. Check phone call rates.


    Is my data safe?

    At Freshworks, we take data security seriously. Our servers are hosted in a world-class data center that is protected by 24-hour surveillance and we ensure that our application is always up-to-date with the latest security patches.

    Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

    Yes, you can. Freshsales is a pay-as-you-go service so you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel at any time. We do not have cancellation fees. But if you cancel, you’ll be billed for the current month.

    Have any other questions?

    If you have questions about Freshsales or the signup process, please email us at support@freshsales.io and we will be glad to answer all your questions.
